Description

Conveniently located near the Supermarket, train station and 7-11 convenience store this unique property has a large garage, heated roof, loft room and plenty of potential bedrooms. All rooms have air-conditioning and auto feeding heaters that don’t need to be refuelled inside the house.

There is space for two cars in the garage or one large sized vehicle. Outside allows for the parking of 3 extra cars, so up to 5 in total. The first floor has two rooms that are currently used as a living room style space. There is a modern full-size bathroom and toilet on the first floor. There is also indoor access to the garage from here. The second floor is the main living area and is a unique circular landing layout with the staircase in the middle allowing you to walk around the the whole floor. There are 3 bedroom areas on the second floor. If the 2 rooms on the first floor are set up as bedrooms then there are potentially 5 bedrooms. The large living/dinning room and kitchen are open plan style and are also on the second floor of the property. There is a 3rd floor loft room that is used as storage.

This property will sell quickly so please only enquire if you are serious and able to move ahead with the purchase immediately.
