SOLD: Akakura Onsen Lodge

Quick information

Hotel location Akakura Onsen
Land area 1871 square meters
Rooms 10
Cost SOLD

Description (To be updated)

This is owned by a university and was used as a dormitory for their students and staff. It is very clean and ready to operate. It has a fully functioning kitchen, dining area with tables, bunk style rooms allowing for numerous guests per room. There are also Japanese tatami mat style bedrooms.

It is a two story building on a large block of land which allows for ample car parking space. The lodge has onsen baths and normal style baths. Onsen would require additonal costs for the onsen rights.

The building is a steel and concrete construction. Two floors with most guest rooms being on the second floor.

A great location down from the main street. A 5 min walk to the lifts.

Additional costs

The following will be additional costs on top of the purchase price, but may be negotiable.

  1. Onsen Rights

  2. Onsen Cost

    The price for onsen rights in Akakura are expensive. Cost will depend on how many pipes you use.

  3. No Onsen
