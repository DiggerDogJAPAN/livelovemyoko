FOR SALE: Nojiri Lake House

Quick information

House location Map
Land area 199 square meters
Rooms Open plan 1 floor
Cost 13.8 million yen
Built 1984

Description

Situated 300m (5min walk) from Lake Nojiri which is visible from the property. The property is raised above a large basement which has a shuttered garage and is a one floor open plan (1SLDK) house. The sloping roof and raised entrance makes snow clearing unnecessary for the the house itself and only the driveway.

The property also comes with various activity equipment such as canoes, trailers etc.

There is space for up to 5 cars to park around the property.
